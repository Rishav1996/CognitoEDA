Customer Dataset Analysis

Dataset Overview

Columns

  • CustomerID: A unique identifier for each customer (from 1 to 200).
  • Gender: The gender of the customer (Male or Female).
  • Age: The age of the customer, which ranges from 18 to 70 years, with an average age of approximately 39.
  • Annual Income (k$): The annual income of the customer in thousands of dollars. It ranges from 15k$ to 137k$, with an average of about 61k$.
  • Spending Score (1-100): A score from 1 to 100 assigned to a customer based on their spending habits. The average score is 50.
